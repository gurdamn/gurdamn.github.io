The 5 Dragon Heads are the leaders of the five distinct "families" that run the separate tenants of the [[The Scale and Fang]] in Dragon's Den within The Shelter. 

## Structure 
- **Type**: Government, Religious, Military, Academic
- **Size**: 5
- **Leadership**: 
	- [[Hothec Roan-P]] (blue) - The "Reptrance" Church of reverence and prayer to Dragonkin 
	- [[Oraese Phaly-P]] (white) - "The Last Ring" Repurposing magical tools
	- [[Aghroh Lun-P]] (black) - "Ralun Institute" Academic inquiry of magic
	- [[Terzu Dreo-P]] (red) - "Aegiswing" Order and safety 
	- [[Aisthiri Xyks-P]] (green) - "Blight Holdings" Economics
